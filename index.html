FAQ

Is it mandatory to have a local director for a Singapore Company??

Yes. You need to have at least 1 local director who is either a natural resident of Singapore or permanent resident or holder of an Employment Pass, EntrePass or a Dependant’s pass, in order to incorporate a company in Singapore.

Is it a must for the owner to be physically present in Singapore to incorporate a company?

It is not required! Our WER1 consultant can do it for you. All you need to do is to provide us with the required documents. Our friendly consultant will guide you through the it.

How long does it take to incorporate a company?

Our experienced consultant can incorporate a company for you within a day. However, you must supply all the necessary documents to us.

What is the minimum amount of share capital required to incorporate a Singapore company?

You can start the company with just S$1 share capital and it is allowed as per provisions of the companies act in Singapore. If you would like to increase share capital in future, you can do so as well , our WER1 consultant will assist you for that if required.

Is it mandatory to open a corporate bank account in Singapore?

It is not mandatory, it will depend on the company's business requirements. if you would like to open corporate bank account, you will have to visit Singapore to do so, along with the majority shareholders and directors of the company. Our WER1 consultant will guide you through the process.
